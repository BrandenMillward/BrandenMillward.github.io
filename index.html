About

Data, strategy, and leadership

I'm a Lead Analyst with a 1st Class BSc (Hons) in Computer Science from Nottingham Trent University and an MSc in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Bath (in progress).

At the Post Office, I lead two teams of analysts on the Horizon improvement programme — identifying system issues, presenting solution options to board-level stakeholders, and designing improvements that benefit both the business and Postmasters' daily lives.

Previously at Capital One, I spent three years as a Strategy and Data Analyst building models, designing monitoring systems, and taking strategy changes from conception through to testing. I'm also a former national-level athlete who stays active through gym and running.
