About me

Download CV

I am a Lead Analyst with a BSc 1st Class Honours Degree in Computer Science from Nottingham Trent University. Currently working at the Post Office Leading 2 teams of analysts as part of the Horizon improvement team, where my role is to identify issues with the Horizon system; present solution options and recommendations to board level stakeholders and work with stakeholder to design and implement improvements that not only benefit the business but the Postmasters daily lives.

Along side my role I am currently working towards achieving an MSc in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Bath

Previous I worked at Capital One for 3 years, working as part of various teams as a Strategy Analyst and Data Analyst with projects ranging from: building and re-grounding models; designing and developing a new monitoring system for the Acquisition team; building various strategy changes from conception through to testing and more.

As part of my undergraduate degree I completed my year industry as a Junior Business Analyst at GKN Driveline to gain industrial experience involving a large about of data analysis and problem solving.

I am also a very active person who is constantly staying active by going to the gym and running, this is from competing in athletics at a national level and many other sports teams throughout my academic career.
